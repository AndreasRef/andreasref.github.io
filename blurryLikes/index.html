Blurry Likes

Blurry Likes is not about connecting people, sharing stunning images, or expressing your true self. Instead it's about the joy of slowly watching the most popular pictures on the platform turn into blurry blobs. A service that dares to ask: "What if likes made things worse?"

Likes gradually turn clear photos into indecipherable, pixelated mysteries. The higher the popularity, the fuzzier the image.

Who needs context?

Not us! There's no text descriptions, no comments, no user profiles (yet!) - just good old-fashioned blur.

Anti-Social Metrics

Likes are all that matter - the more you get, the less you see. It's the ultimate anti-climax.

Alpha version

We're currently in the alpha stage, operating on an invite-only basis. Visitors have the privilege of liking images, but uploading is not yet an option. Want your most popular images vanish into the blurry abyss? Contact Andreas Refsgaard for access.
